# Arka Tea Traders – Bank Reconciliation Assignment

## Overview

This repository contains the bank reconciliation analysis for Arka Tea Traders using the provided HDFC bank statement and Zoho ledger export.

The objective of this assignment is to identify discrepancies between the bank statement and accounting ledger, prepare a reconciliation statement, and recommend corrective journal entries.

---

## Files Included

### Data

* HDFC Bank Statement (PDF)
* Zoho Ledger Export (CSV)

### Analysis

* Discrepancy Register identifying mismatches between bank and ledger

### Reports

* Bank Reconciliation Statement summarizing all reconciling items

### Journal Entries

* Correcting accounting entries required to resolve discrepancies

### Memo

* Brief memo to the Finance Head summarizing key findings and recommended actions

---

## Key Findings

* Opening balance mismatch identified
* Timing differences due to bank charges and delayed entries
* Required journal corrections documented

---

## Outcome

The reconciliation ensures that the ledger balances align with the bank statement and that all discrepancies are properly documented and corrected.
